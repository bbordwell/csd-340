Hobbies

Astronomy

I have always loved astronomy. As a kid I had a small telescope and a pair of binoculars that I enjoyed using. When I was older I continued to enjoy just looking at the stars, but I did not have the money to pursue it further. Eventually I had enough money that I got back into it and got nice telescope as well as better binoculars. I also combined this interest with my interest in photography and started doing astrophotography.

Camping

My wife and I love to go camping. We have been camping all over the United States. We mostly do car camping, but we have done a litte backpacking as well. We only ever camp in a tent, and we have camped in weather between 20 and 110 degrees.

Photography

I enjoy photography as well. I have done portraits before, my favorite types of photography are astrophotography and nature photography. These two types of photography go well together with my other hobbies of astronomy and camping.
